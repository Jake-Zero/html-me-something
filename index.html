The Runaway Guys

  • Chuggaconroy

Chuggaconroy was Donkey Kong in Mario Party 1-4, Daisy in 5-6, and Toad starting from 7. Proton Jon played Mario until 3 where he consistently played as Waluigi. NintendoCaprisun has played as Yoshi all throughout the Mario Party games and considering the green dinosaur hasn't been out of the picture since the first Mario Party, it's doubtful that that'll change anytime soon.

ProtonJon

ProtonJon is a variety streamer on Twitch. He generally streams on Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday. On Wednesday and Friday, he generally streams whatever he or his followers on Twitter want to. On Mondays, he usually does Game Clearing, which he's not going to clear his stretch goal of A-Z this year. His recently cleared game was Resident Evil: Director's Cut before he left for his bi-yearly TRG recording. Saturdays, though, are his bread and butter: Fortune Cookie Streams. He starts the stream with whatever game he feels like playing at the time for 30 minutes at least, then he puts up a cookie where he pulls 5 games from his ginormous collection of games, RPG and tactics games excluded, with few exceptions, and his audience votes on what he should play next. If he has co-comms with him, they get a choice as well. He will then put a timer for 1:30 to determine what gets played next. If the game in the lead is very far ahead of the pack, he will declare it a Mercy Kill and that game will automatically be played next. If it's a co-comm game that wins, they can't pick a game next round. If a game in 2nd place is 1% behind the game in the lead before the timer goes off, that game will automatically be put into the next round of cookie. Before going to the next game, he'll go to Intermission, where he will read out subscriptions, give out created eggs to said subs, and read donations. Afterwards, he'll look at art in what he calls "Art Block". These streams are my particular favorite for the kind of shit people are willing to put Jon through, either through art or games.
